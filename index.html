Jesus

A Righteous Role Model of the Highest Good

His Holy Words, Divine Actions, and Righteous Will:

Ask, and it will be given to you.
Seek, and you will find.
Knock, and the door will be opened to you.

A good tree cannot bear bad fruit, nor can a bad tree bear good fruit.
Every tree that does not bear good fruit is cut down and thrown into the fire.
Therefore by their fruits, you will know them.

Be Righteous, Grateful, and Generous to all People.
Be Wise, Trustworthy, and Forgiving of all People.
Be Courageous, Compassionate, and Loving to all People.

Thy kingdom come.

Thy will be done,

on Earth, as it is in Heaven.
