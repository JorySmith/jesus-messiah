Jesus

A Righteous Role Model of the Highest Good

Ask, and it will be given to you.
Seek, and you will find.
Knock, and the door will be opened to you.
For the Son of Man did not come to be served,
but to serve, and to give his life as a ransom for many.

A good tree cannot bear bad fruit, nor can a bad tree bear good fruit. Every tree that does not
bear good fruit, is cut down and thrown into the fire. Therefore, by their fruits, you will know them.

For wide is the gate and broad is the way that leads to destruction.
Because narrow is the gate and difficult is the way which leads to life.

At all times, you are either serving
the Highest Good or the lowest evil.
To overcome evil, you must see it.
To seek God, the Highest Good, you must aim for it.
To sin, is to miss your aim.
How can we ever seek righteousness if we deny sin?
How can we ever be forgiven if we deny our sins?
Jesus's love and forgiveness are unlimited.
But to receive it, you must see your sins.

Seek the Highest Good by being:
Righteous, Grateful, and Generous to all People, like Him.
Wise, Trustworthy, and Forgiving to all People, like Him.
Courageous, Compassionate, and Loving to all People, like Him.
And avoiding the nine deadly sins: wickedness, cowardice,
pride, greed, wrath, envy, lust, gluttony, and sloth, like Him.

Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart, like Him.

Thy Kingdom come.
Thy Will be done,
on Earth as it is in Heaven.
