Jesus

A Righteous Role Model of the Highest Good

Ask, and it will be given to you.
Seek, and you will find.
Knock, and the door will be opened to you.

For the Son of Man did not come to be served,
but to serve, and to give his life as a
ransom for many.

A good tree cannot bear bad fruit,
nor can a bad tree bear good fruit.
Every tree that does not bear good fruit,
is cut down and thrown into the fire.
Therefore, by their fruits, you will know them.

For wide is the gate and broad is the way
that leads to destruction. Because narrow is
the gate and difficult is the way which leads to life.

Be Righteous, Grateful, and Generous to all People.
Be Wise, Trustworthy, and Forgiving of all People.
Be Courageous, Compassionate, and Loving to all People.
Whatever you do, work at it with all your heart.

Thy kingdom come.
Thy will be done,
on Earth as it is in Heaven.
